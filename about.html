Lian's meaning

Lian Coffee is a passion project. The name Lian is a chinese name which means graceful or lotus flower. Lian Coffee has been designed for your everyday activity for you to be boosted and ready to attack your day. Working with experts allowed me to create aromatic flavours and create the best coffee for you.
